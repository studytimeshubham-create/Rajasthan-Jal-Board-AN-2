# DETAILED SUMMARY OF WATER TARIFF 2025

This document summarises all water charges, general charges, connection charges, security deposits, sewerage taxes, and penalties as notified by the Public Health Engineering Department, Rajasthan, effective from the month following April 10, 2025.

---

## PART A: WATER CHARGES

### 1. Domestic Water Rates (Connection Size: 15 mm to 25 mm)
Charges are applied on a slab basis per 1,000 litres of monthly consumption:

| Slab | Monthly Consumption | Rate (Rs./1,000 L) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| a | Up to first 8,000 litres | Rs. 7.00 |
| b | 8,001 – 15,000 litres | Rs. 9.00 |
| c | 15,001 – 40,000 litres | Rs. 18.00 |
| d | Above 40,000 litres | Rs. 22.00 |

**Note:** No water charges for consumers with functional water meters and monthly consumption up to 15,000 litres for 15 mm connections only.

---

### 2. Minimum Monthly Charges – Domestic Category (15 mm to 25 mm)

| Connection Size | Condition | Charge (Rs./Month) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 15 mm | Consumption up to 8 KL | Rs. 88.00 |
| 15 mm | Consumption more than 8 KL | Rs. 220.00 |
| 20 mm | — | Rs. 880.00 |
| 25 mm | — | Rs. 2,200.00 |

**Note:** * Minimum charges apply only when monthly consumption exceeds 15,000 litres.
* Minimum charges calculated on average consumption of last 6 months.

#### Who Qualifies as a Domestic Consumer?
The domestic category includes residential houses and the following:
* All Government educational institutions
* Places of worship (temples, mosques, churches, etc.)
* Orphanages/Leper homes/Yatim Khana registered with Social Justice & Empowerment Department
* Government Hospitals, Dispensaries and Anganwari Kendra
* Hostels run by Education, Tribal Welfare, Social Justice & Empowerment Departments of GoR/GoI
* Jails, Children Rehabilitation Homes, Mahila Ashrams operated by SJ&ED of GoR/GoI
* Small shops/offices (up to 200 sq.ft built-up area) using water only for drinking and sanitary purposes

## PART C: LATE PAYMENT SURCHARGE AND ROUND-OFF

| Item | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| Late Payment Surcharge (LPS) – within 2 months | 10% of total water bill amount |
| LPS – beyond 2 months | LPS added to bill + 18% interest on outstanding amount |
| Annual Tariff Increment | All charges multiplied by a factor of 1.10 every year from 1st April |
| Bill Round-Off | Total water bill rounded off to nearest next Rupee |
